About TechyWorld

What is TechyWorld?

TechyWorld is a web-based Online Examination System designed to help students and programmers enhance their skills through carefully curated questions and assessments.

Key Features:
  • Easy Registration and Secure Login
  • Well-structured Online Exams with Timer
  • Instant Evaluation and Result Display
  • User Profile and History Management

Our mission is to make learning more interactive and testing more practical, helping users to prepare for coding interviews, exams, and competitive programming.
